Services

  • COMMUNITY SERVICE (CHARITABLE WORK)

    Off our profits, personal skills, individual donations and partner resources; we shall continuously be identifying humanitarian needs in our community and provide support to them.

  • SAVINGS AND CREDIT SCHEME

    The saving discipline is currently emphasized through our saving scheme. We shall later expand and introduce a Savings and Credit Corporative Scheme (EYCSCS), with the principle objecting of encouraging savings and providing capital to members. Each member will be obliged to contribute and participate in the EYCSCS. This will help generate funds but also provide a sense of shared responsibility on the matter. The EYCSCS is expected to be registered by October 2019.

    Membership : $50-$100 Monthly
